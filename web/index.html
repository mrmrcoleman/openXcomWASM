HOW IT WORKS

🌐

Runs in your browser

OpenXcom compiled to WebAssembly. No plugins, no extensions, no install. Works in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.

🔒

Your data stays local

Game files are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your saves never leave your machine.

💾

Persistent saves

Save your game and come back later — saves persist across browser sessions. Export and import saves to transfer between devices.

💰

Bring your own data

You need a legitimate copy of X-COM: UFO Defense. Upload your game files once and play forever. Available on Steam for under $5.

GETTING STARTED

  1. Get the game data. You need the original X-COM: UFO Defense files. Buy it on Steam or GOG if you don't already own it.
  2. Click "Play Now" and upload your XCOM data folder when prompted. On Steam, it's typically at: steamapps/common/XCom UFO Defense/XCOM/
  3. Play! Your data is cached in the browser — you only need to upload once. Saves persist automatically and can be exported as backups.

FAQ

  1. Is this legal?
    Yes. OpenXcom is open-source (GPL). You provide your own legally-purchased game data. No copyrighted assets are distributed by this site.
  2. Where is my data stored?
    Everything stays in your browser's local IndexedDB storage. No data is sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will remove it.
  3. Can I transfer saves between devices?
    Yes! Use the gear menu (top-right) during gameplay to export saves as a .zip file. Import them on another device using the same menu.
  4. Does it work on mobile?
    The engine runs, but X-COM was designed for mouse input. A desktop or laptop browser is recommended for the best experience.
